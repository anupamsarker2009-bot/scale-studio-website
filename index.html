FAQ

Frequntly asked questions

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

We work with both startups and established companies. Our approach is flexible and tailored to the specific needs and stage of each business. For startups, we focus on building strong foundations and achieving efficient, early growth.

Do you offer development or only design?

We offer a full spectrum of services, including both design and development. We provide end-to-end solutions, from initial concept and user experience design all the way through to full-scale engineering, deployment, and maintenance.

What does Hypen actually specialize in?

Hypen focuses on product design, website experience design, and scalable UI systems. The core value is structuring products and platforms so teams can ship faster, convert better, and scale without design debt.

What industries do you usually work with?

Our core methodologies—focused on user-centric design, scalable development, and strategic growth—are applicable to virtually any sector. Rather than limiting ourselves to specific verticals, we prioritize partnering.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope, complexity, and specific goals. A straightforward website or prototype might take 4-8 weeks, while a comprehensive platform or custom application can range from 3 to 6 months or more.

What does your design process look like?

Our design process is a collaborative and flexible journey focused on solving real problems for users and your business. It begins with a deep dive to understand your goals, challenges, and audience. We then move through stages of exploration.

What does your design process look like?

Yes, absolutely. You will work directly with the designer or design team assigned to your project. We believe transparent and collaborative communication is essential for success. You’ll have regular contact through scheduled meetings, reviews.
